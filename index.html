What is the Pomodoro Technique?

The Pomodoro technique is a well-researched time-management tool. You set the timer to 25 and you study, research, or anything productive that needs to be done. Then you take a break.

Why use the Pomodoro Technique?

It is a well-researched technique that maximizes productivity in studying and working.
